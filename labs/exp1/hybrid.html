  1. To perform the simulation, follow the below given steps:
  2. 1) Select the topology from the dropdown that you would like to implement.
  3. 2) A blank rectangular area is provided which defines the working area.
  4. 3) A series of components are given along with their labels.
  5. 4) In order to construct a topology, first select any of the components and then immediately click on the working area to place it.
  6. 5) To draw a cable in between any two components, first select the desired cable, then click on the first component and then immediately click on the second component.
  7. 6) Once the topology is constructed, click on the check button to verify if the constructed topology is built correctly or not.
  8. 7) In order to reset the simulation, click on the reset button.
